The Mediasite Project is recruiting

Paid roles are available to assist in the roll-out of Mediasite (audio and slide recording of lectures) during the 2015/16 academic year. The positions (Recording Support Assistant and Recording Support Coordinator) are open to current students; apply now to put money in your pocket and bring benefit to student learning at the same time.

Spotlight

As a partner in the Festival of Ideas, the University of Bristol has negotiated a special rate for its students and staff for most events in the festival. Upcoming events include Nobel Prize-winning economist Alvin Roth (13 July; free) and writers William Boyd (14 Sept) and Salman Rushdie (11 Oct).
Spotlight

The University has recently launched a new visitor wireless service, ‘_The Cloud’. You may find that your device automatically connects to this service rather than eduroam, which is the preferred service for Bristol students and staff. Advice on how to avoid automatically connecting to The Cloud is available.
